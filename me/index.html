Research

My general research involves Natural Language Processing and Deep Learning.

At SEU, I focus on Entity Linking, that is the task of determining the identity of entities mentioned in text. Potential application includes Information Extraction, Information Retrieval, Content Analysis, Question Answering, Knowledge Base Population, etc.

At 17zuoye, I focus on Automated Essay Scoring, along with Spell Checking and Grammar Error Correction. As a top online education platform for K-12 students, 17zuoye is committed to improve learning experience for Chinese students. We are dedicated to build an advanced Intelligent Tutoring System to achieve that goal.
